My Favorite Hobby
Playing Chess

My favorite hobby is to play chess for many reasons. Firstly, it sharpens your mind and makes you think of strategies out of the box. You have to think a lot about every move or you end up losing. In addition, you get to play with other people and have fun with them. You can make more friends like this! Lastly, you don't go on a screen and you get time off electronics which we have too much of these days. "Life is nothing but a great game of chess, and every person we encounter is a piece on the board." -Allpastetimes.com
